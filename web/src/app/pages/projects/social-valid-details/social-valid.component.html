Social Valid - Lead Technology Engineer

December 2021 - March 2025

As of March 2025, Social Valid LLC has been shut down due to changing markets and business/financial needs. This page documents what the Social Valid application was, and showcases some of my contributions and achievements while working there.

Overview

Social Valid was a web application built with the goal of providing users a safe place to house all of their socials and web links, and prove that they are legitimate through multiple verification methods. Offering verification badges, security, theme customization, and a variety of page types to suit every user's needs, Social Valid hopes to become the home of trusted information on the web.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Partnered directly with the CEO to establish key strategic goals, technical scoping, success roadmaps, and delivery timelines.
  • Implemented numerous features and optimizations in both the front-end and back-end.
  • Created detailed project proposals and UI mock-ups for upcoming features.
  • Collaborated with QA team to ensure high test coverage and timely resolution of tracked issues.
  • Responsible for creating, budgeting, and maintaining all AWS infrastructure across both production and testing environments.
  • Responsible for creating and assigning tasks to all team members for each Agile sprint cycle.
  • Responsible for training, mentoring, and conducting peer review with team members.
  • Responsible for maintaining versioning consistency and executing the initial product launch, in addition to dozens of subsequent feature roll-outs.

Notable Contributions and Achievements

  • Optimized AWS infrastructure for existing projects to reduce hosting cost by 87%.
  • Integrated identity verification and social verification through multiple external APIs and OAuth providers.
  • Optimized database structures, reducing lookup times by severalfold and improving query performance.
  • Designed and implemented a flexible theming and layout customization system for Social Valid user pages.
  • Implemented a live payment system with subscription tracking, cancellation, and refunds.
  • Designed and implemented backend solutions for multi-level user permissions management.
  • Developed a standalone admin dashboard for comprehensive database management and oversight.
  • Developed team best practices for bug testing and process improvement.
